# Sharding vs Partitioning

## Overview

Sharding and partitioning are both database scaling techniques, but they work differently. Understanding their differences and trade-offs is crucial for choosing the right approach for your system.

## Prerequisites

Understanding of database scaling concepts and distributed systems.
