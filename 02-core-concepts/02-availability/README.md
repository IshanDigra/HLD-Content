# Availability

## Overview

Availability is the percentage of time a system is operational and accessible to users. It's measured as uptime and is crucial for user experience and business continuity.

## Prerequisites

Understanding of system reliability concepts and basic networking.

## Notes

Availability is often measured in "nines" (99.9% = three nines). Each additional nine requires significantly more investment and complexity. Choose the right level based on your business requirements.
